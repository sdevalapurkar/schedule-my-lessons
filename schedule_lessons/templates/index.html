About Schedulearn

Schedulearn is all about connecting students and professors in a way that hasn't been done before. The way Schedulearn does that is by giving tutors the ability to give students assignments, schedule lessons and much more.

Students get access to features that enhance their learning experience such as chatrooms with other students, the ability to schedule lessons with their teacher and more.
